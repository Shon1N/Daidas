Why PNB Training?

Professional Nail & Beauty Acadamy is proud to be a memeber of APPETD. PNB Acadamy's comprehensive courses will enhance the student to master the techniques and the skills of nail and beauty applications. This will include intensive theory and practical training. Intensive training ensures that the student is capable and confident to achieve their full potential and to be able to continue on their own or in a professional salon environment. Students will be issued with a certificate once competency evaluation has been completed and achieved. All courses include a professional training kit and full course manual.

All Beauty Training Courses

Crystal Acrylic Liquid (4 days) R 3750

The course includes everything you will need to learn about acrylic nails. You will learn to produce the perfect combination of acrylic lyquid and powder and apply it to the different areas of the nail with a brush. By combining acrylic liquid and powder a strong flexible overlay can be produced. This versatile overlay can be applied to the natural nail tip: it can also be sculpted over a form.

Silk & Fibre (2 Days) R 2250

The course includes everything you need to learn about fibre/silk nails. This is the application process of sticky fibre/silk strips directly to the nail. Thin layers of resin are applied with the help of an activator to set the resin and make the fibre wave invisible, and to strengthen and enhance the nail shape. Fibreglass overlays can be used over a tip or to strengthen the natural nail. It is an excellent remedy for broken or cracked nails as it provides a thin yet very strong coating. As fibreglass is water resistant, it is a good choice for clients working with water or those who are allergic to UV gel or acrylic.

Crystal Acrylic Dip (1.5 days) R 2250

One of the latest advancements in nail technology. The dip System includes a combination of Resin and Acrylic to quickly create nails that are durable and flexible. This sysytem is suitable for all nail types.

UV Gel Systems (3 Days) R 3500

Gel is the latest innovation in the fast-growing nail industry. Gel is slightly easier to apply than acrylic because you do not have to mix powder and liquid. It is odour free and harsdwearing. A wide range of gels are available to suit the need of both the client and the technician.
